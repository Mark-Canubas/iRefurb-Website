Our Refurbishment Process

Quality You Can Trust

1

Sourcing & Inspection

We partner with trusted suppliers and carriers to source premium pre-owned devices. Each device undergoes a different set of inspection to ensure it meets the quality standards.

We only accept devices that have been properly decommissioned and have no history of liquid damage or unauthorized modifications.

2

Comprehensive Diagnostics

Every device undergoes a thorough diagnostic test covering all hardware components including display, cameras, sensors, buttons, speakers, microphones, and connectivity features.

3

Quality Control & Certification

Before packaging, each device undergoes a final quality control check. We test all features and ensure the device meets our strict performance standards.

Every device is factory reset, updated to the latest iOS version, and receives our certification of quality.
